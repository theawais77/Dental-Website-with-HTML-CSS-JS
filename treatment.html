Treaments We do

At Tooth Wiz we offer a wide range of treatments to help you achieve your smile.
These are the treatments provided by us

Root Canal

A root canal is performed when the endodontist removes the infected pulp and nerve in the root of the tooth, cleans and shapes the inside of the root canal, then fills and seals the space.

Teeth whitening

Teeth whitening is a procedure to lighten the color of your teeth and remove stains. You can have it done at a dentist's office, or do it yourself at home.

Teeth Extraction

A tooth extraction is a dental procedure during which your tooth is completely removed from its socket. Sometimes, people refer to this as â€œpulling" a tooth.

Dentures

Dentures are removable appliances used to replace missing teeth. They improve chewing ability and enhance facial appearance for individuals with significant tooth loss.

Mouthguards

Customized mouthguards protect teeth from grinding (bruxism) and prevent injuries during sports activities. They are designed for comfort and optimal protection..

Dental Crowns

Dental crowns are tooth-shaped caps that cover damaged or decayed teeth. They restore the tooth's shape, size, and strength, improving both function and aesthetics. .

Dental Sealants

Dental sealants are thin, protective coatings applied to the chewing surfaces of molars and premolars. They help prevent cavities by sealing out bacteria and food particles from the grooves of the teeth..

Emergency Dental Care

Emergency dental care services address urgent dental issues, such as severe tooth pain, broken teeth, or dental trauma.

Dental Bonding:

Dental bonding involves the application of a tooth-colored resin to repair chipped, cracked, or discolored teeth.
